My first Blog Post

November 23, 2017 / by TheSeeker

I knew exactly what love looked like,
Rewind a few many months,
I was there and he was there,
I was hooked by not more than a few words,
It was him and I and many conversations later,

Each time I heard his ping ring,
It couldn't be worse,
He was beautiful for Better or for worse,
He had me falling, inside out.
He hid a terrible secret, like most men do,
Whose revelation brought about my heart’s great storm,
There was no more of him and I,
Just memories that hurt more than just a pang.
Then came long months,
Of icy cold,
The winter was colder than most,
Brace I did my freezing heart,
Refusing all warmth that came with promises divine.
Fast forward a few many months,
Here I am right where I started,
And I know not why,
Like a moth to the streetlight,
I am drawn to my favourite pain,
This passionate destructive love of mine.
